# Your 8 Custom Claude Skills - Complete Guide

This document describes all 8 skills created for your workflow.

## Skills Overview

### 1. Idea Validator
**Purpose:** Systematically evaluate business ideas and product concepts

**Key Features:**
- 7-point validation framework (Problem, Solution, Market, Competition, Business Model, Risk, Resources)
- Structured output with scores and recommendations
- Industry-specific validation questions for B2B, B2C, SaaS, marketplace, hardware, AI/ML, and platform ideas

**When to use:** "Validate this idea", "Is this a good business?", "Should I build this?"

**What it does:**
- Analyzes problem-solution fit
- Evaluates market opportunity
- Assesses competitive landscape
- Reviews business model viability
- Identifies key risks
- Provides actionable next steps

## How to Use These Skills

### Installation
1. Download the .skill files
2. In Claude, go to Settings → Skills
3. Click "Upload Skill"
4. Select each .skill file
5. Skills are now active!

### Usage
Just naturally ask Claude to perform tasks related to these skills:
- "Help me validate my business idea about..."
- "Plan a product launch for..."
- "Design a user flow for..."
- "Write ad copy for..."
- "Create a PRD for..."
- "Organize these meeting notes..."
- "Review this code..."
- "Draft an email to..."

Claude will automatically use the appropriate skill based on your request.

### Customization
Skills can be customized:
1. Download and unzip the .skill file (it's a zip with .skill extension)
2. Edit SKILL.md and reference files
3. Re-package using the package_skill.py script
4. Upload the modified skill

## Tips for Getting the Most from Skills

1. **Be specific**: "Create a launch plan for a SaaS product launching in 30 days" works better than "help me launch"

2. **Provide context**: Share relevant details about your product, audience, or constraints

3. **Iterate**: Ask Claude to refine or adjust the output based on your feedback

4. **Combine skills**: Use multiple skills in sequence (e.g., validate idea → plan launch → write marketing copy)

5. **Customize**: Modify skills to match your company's specific processes or terminology

6. **Reference outputs**: Ask Claude to create outputs in your preferred format (markdown, docx, etc.)
