# Video 01: My AI Agent Doesn't Just Retry — It Escalates

## Hook (0-5s)
"When my AI agent fails, it doesn't just try the same thing twice."

## Demo (5-40s)
"It runs a strategy ladder."

"First — quick fix. Minimal change. Two shots at it."

"If that fails — it reads more context and refactors the approach."

"Still failing? — Completely different strategy. Alternative solution."

"And if nothing works — it writes me a detailed failure report so I'm not starting from zero."

## Result (40-50s)
"Four strategies. Five max attempts. Fifteen minute hard timeout."

"It logs every attempt so next time — it skips straight to what worked."
