# Local Property / Permit Lead Template

## Context
This template is for outreach to local contractors, developers, and property owners
in Broward and Miami-Dade counties who have active building permits or recently
purchased commercial properties.

## Scoring Adjustments
- Local projects get +10 bonus (face-to-face meetings possible)
- Commercial/multi-family > $500K construction value: +15 bonus
- Repeat clients (same owner/contractor in database): +20 bonus

## Follow-up Cadence
1. Initial outreach: within 48 hours of permit filing
2. Follow-up #1: 5 business days after initial
3. Follow-up #2: 2 weeks after initial (if no response)
4. Archive after 30 days with no response
